What is Spot-It?
Spot-It is an online community where users submit, vote and comment on content that
has to do with Music. Users can also get the latest news and music activities. Spotify is integrated within
Spot-It to allow users to share their playlists, create new playlists. It's the best of both worlds, an online
community and Spotify.
